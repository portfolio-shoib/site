My Startup Journey

🚀 My Startup Journey

From Business Student to Service Founder

This is the story of how I turned a university idea into a growing, client-ready service — balancing practical experience, digital tools, and a clear mission to help small businesses grow.

📘 Phase 1: Learning the Foundations

  • Studying Business Administration at Virtual University

  • Exploring tools like SWOT analysis and Zoho Books

  • Noticed local shopkeepers struggling with basic records

💡 Realized there was a gap between business education and real-world small business needs.

🧪 Phase 2: Testing the Idea

  • Helped my first local client (a grocery store) track daily sales

  • Built custom Excel sheets and used WhatsApp for updates

  • Tested a 7-day free trial model to build trust

🎯 Simple tools + personal attention = strong client feedback.

📦 Phase 3: Launching the Service

  • Officially launched my record-keeping and strategy service

  • Printed flyers, created WhatsApp Business profile, and set up templates

  • Managed 5 trial clients using voice notes, photos, and reports

🛠️ Focused on clarity, simplicity, and support — not complexity.

📈 Phase 4: Expanding Offerings

  • Added SWOT coaching, visual strategy kits, and AI-based tools

  • Delivered Notion dashboards and GPT-based content assistants

  • Trained a helper to assist with data collection and client visits

🌱 Started building scalable systems, not just one-off tasks.

🧭 Phase 5: Scaling Smart

  • Standardized client onboarding and reporting workflow

  • Started segmenting clients by type (retail, service, eCommerce)

  • Added client testimonials and created a structured referral system

  • Introduced optional add-ons like inventory, costing, and GPT toolkits

📊 Focused on building repeatable systems and refining client experience.

🌟 Today

  • Serving small businesses with affordable, custom support

  • Mixing bookkeeping, AI tools, and strategic thinking

  • Still learning, growing, and building — one client at a time

👣 This journey is just getting started.
