📊 Shoib’s Bookkeeping Startup

Tagline: Your Partner in Financial Clarity

My startup offers personalized, low-tech bookkeeping services designed for small businesses, combining in-person visits with digital efficiency.

  • WhatsApp-Driven: Share data via voice, text, or photos.
  • Zoho-Powered: Secure, professional record-keeping.
  • Internal Focus: No tax filings, just clear records.
  • In-Person Support: Regular visits to build trust.

Core Services

  • Daily Entries: Track sales, expenses, and cash flow effortlessly.
  • Monthly Reports: Bilingual PDF summaries sent via WhatsApp.
  • Receipt Organization: Digital folders for all your proofs.
  • Data Collection: Flexible voice/photo submissions via WhatsApp.
  • Privacy Guaranteed: No external sharing or tax involvement.
  • Future Add-Ons: Inventory, payroll, and costing tools.
